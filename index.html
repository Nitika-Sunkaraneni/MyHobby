About

My hobby is making Art. I really enjoy doing Art. I like drawing, painting, and coloring! I have created lots of drawings! I drew a dragon, tiger, bunny, a buddha meditating next to a waterfall, and much more! I have also created lots of paintings. I painted a waterfall, the Effiel Tower, a beach with a sunset, a rose in a mystical place, and more! I have been doing art for a very long time. Recently, I got into digital art. Digital art is making art on a device, like a tablet or ipad, but I still do art on paper too. I think Art is a really fun and good hobby for me! :D
